Reasons Why Crypto Holds Power

Reason 1 – It cannot be counterfeited

Unlike credit cards, Crypto cannot be reversed back to the sender neither can it be counterfeited. The technology lies in cryptography and this allows you to sign the operations that are carried out all the interconnected network. This prevents all fraudulent operations.

Reason 2 – It is recognized on a universal level

Crypto can be used at international levels as it is now being widely recognized even for international payment. It is not bound by exchange rates, interest rates, transaction charges, or specific country charges. The payment structure is such that the crypto coins are held in wallets while the payment transactions are collected in blocks.

Just like John Mcafee, Founder of Mcafee said, "You can't stop things like Bitcoin. It will be everywhere and the world will have to readjust. World governments will have to readjust".

Reason 3 – There is no risk of identity theft

Blockchain, the technology upon which crypto lies, prevents identity theft in 3 ways:

  • It lets you own your data by keeping your online information secure. It automatically signals you in the event of any fraudulent attempt.
  • It enhances its fraud entry barrier. It eliminates the need for a centralized authority and strengthens its network security to a point where it is impenetrable.
  • It secures transactions you make online through cards. It would not allow any transaction you make online to be successful unless it is verified as legitimate.

Reason 4- Ituses a decentralized technology

In this way, no single person or group has control; rather, it is collectively controlled by all users. A global network of computers jointly manages the database. With the use of decentralized technology, the database records transactions on a user-to-user basis.

Reason 5 – It has Lower fees

Another good thing about crypto is that there are usually no transaction fees for crypto exchanges. This is because the miners are already compensated by the network. Isn't that just great?

Reason 6 – There is access to the market for everyone

The best news is that everyone can access this digital market. With billions of people all over the world, crypto serves as a better means to access exchanges and perform all transactions. It is safe, secured and is not bound by country charges.

Crypto IRA

We already talked extensively about IRAs in our previous articles on gold. Just like we have Precious metal IRAs, a Crypto IRA is a self-directed IRA that can hold any cryptocurrency ranging from Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Ripple, and every other coin.

Experts would always advise you to have a portfolio that is diversified. Converting part of your retirement savings to Bitcoin or Ethereum can reduce your portfolio volatility as well as yield potential large returns in the future.

Transfer or Rollover

Like we discussed in the previous article, there are two ways to fund your Crypto IRA. The first alternative is always to opt for a transfer from your current IRA account. Here, the asset flows from custodian to another, and the great thing about this is that you can do it as many times as you want.

Your second alternative is a rollover like that of a 401k to crypto IRA rollover. Here, a rollover is only possible after you must have received a distribution from your already existing IRA. Then you deposit the funds into a new IRA custodian account, and this is only possible within 60 days, after which you would incur taxes and penalties on your withdrawal. Unlike the transfer option, you may only roll over your funds once in 12 months.
